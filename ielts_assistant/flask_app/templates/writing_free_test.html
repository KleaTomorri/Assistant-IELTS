IELTS General Writing Test - Task 1

Given Situation:

You recently moved to a new city and would like to invite a friend to visit you. Write a letter to your friend explaining why they should visit and what you have planned for their stay.

Instructions:

  • Write at least 150 words.
  • Include reasons why your friend should visit.
  • Mention any plans or activities you have for their stay.

IELTS General Writing Test - Task 2

Given Topic:

Some people think that the best way to increase road safety is to increase the minimum legal age for driving cars or motorbikes. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Instructions:

  • Write at least 250 words.
  • Present your opinion clearly.
  • Provide arguments and examples to support your view.
